Astroid shooter

to build -- cabal build
to run -- cabal run

you play a character that you can move up and down the left side of the screen.
Enemies will start spawning on the right side of the screen and shoot bullets at you.
Dodge these bullets and fire your own to destroy the enemies.
your health points are displayed in the top left of the screen.
defeat the enemies and try to beat your highscore!

If desired, you can also make it harder or easier for yourself by changing the spawn rate of the enemies.

Controls:

W -- Move up
S -- Move down

P -- Pause the game

K -- Make enemies spawn less frequent
J -- Make enemies spawn more frequent

L -- Shoot bullet
